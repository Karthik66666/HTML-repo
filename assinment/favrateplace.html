Favourite Place

Bathrinath temple

Badarinath or Badarinarayana Temple is a Hindu temple dedicated to Vishnu. It is situated in the town of Badrinath in Uttarakhand, India. The temple is also one of the 108 Divya Desams dedicated to Vishnu-holy shrines for Vaishnavas-who is worshipped as Badrinath. It is open for six months every year (between the end of April and the beginning of November), because of extreme weather conditions in the Himalayan region.

Norway

Norway is known for its stunning natural landscapes, rich cultural heritage, and historical landmarks rather than palaces specifically. While it doesn't have palaces that are commonly associated with monarchies like Buckingham Palace in the United Kingdom or the Palace of Versailles in France, Norway does have several notable royal residences and historic buildings that hold significance.

Germany

Germany is a diverse and fascinating country with a lot to offer. It has a rich history, vibrant cities, beautiful landscapes, and a thriving cultural scene. Whether you're interested in exploring its historical landmarks, experiencing its festivals, enjoying its renowned cuisine, or immersing yourself in its art and music, Germany has something for everyone. From the bustling streets of Berlin to the charming towns along the Rhine River, there is a wide range of experiences to enjoy. So, if Germany is your favorite place, you have plenty to look forward to on your future visits or in continuing to explore all that the country has to offer.
